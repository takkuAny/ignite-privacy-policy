# Privacy Policy for Ignite

**Last updated: March 21, 2026**

## Overview

Ignite ("we", "our", or "the app") is an English learning app that helps you practice explaining technical documents by voice. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## Data We Collect

### 1. Account Information
- When you sign in with Apple, we receive a unique user identifier and, if you choose to share it, your email address.
- This information is stored in our database (Supabase) to manage your account and subscription.

### 2. Voice Data
- The app uses your device's microphone to capture your spoken answers.
- Audio is processed locally on your device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework (SFSpeechRecognizer).
- **We do not store or transmit raw audio recordings.**
- Transcribed text is sent to our AI service (Claude by Anthropic) for evaluation and is not retained after processing.

### 3. Usage Data
- We track the number of question generations and evaluations per month to manage free-tier usage limits.
- This data is stored in our database and associated with your account.

### 4. Learning History
- Session results (scores and feedback) are stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
- This data does not leave your device unless you explicitly export it.

### 5. Subscription Information
- Subscription status is managed through RevenueCat and Apple's in-app purchase system.
- We store your subscription plan ("free" or "pro") in our database to provide the appropriate level of service.
- We do not have access to your payment details, which are handled entirely by Apple.

| Data | Purpose |

## Data Retention

- Account and usage data is retained while your account is active.
- You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.
- Local learning history can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
